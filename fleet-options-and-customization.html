Fleet Options and Customization

What types of yachts are available for charter during the Formula 1 Abu Dhabi event?

A variety of yachts are available, including luxury motor yachts, sailing yachts, and superyachts, each offering different amenities and capacities to suit various preferences and needs.

Can I customize the interior or facilities of the yacht for my charter?

Yes, many yacht charters offer customization options including personalized decor, specific dining arrangements, and additional amenities such as water sports equipment or entertainment systems.

Are there options for branding or theming the yacht for corporate events during the race weekend?

Yes, you can often arrange for branding opportunities with banners, custom flags, and themed decorations to align with your corporate identity or event theme.

Is it possible to choose a specific crew or chef based on preferences?

Many yacht charters allow you to request specific crew members or chefs who specialize in certain cuisines or have experience catering to particular guest preferences.

How flexible is the itinerary when chartering a yacht for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix?

The itinerary is generally flexible within agreed terms; you can plan your schedule around race viewing times and other activities while ensuring docking arrangements comply with marina regulations.
