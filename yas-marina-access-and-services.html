What is included in the Yas Marina access for a Formula 1 Abu Dhabi yacht charter?

Yas Marina access typically includes mooring for your yacht, VIP passes to the marinas exclusive areas, and premium viewing spots for the race. Additional services like catering, boat transfer shuttles, and concierge services might also be offered.

How can I book a berth at Yas Marina during the Formula 1 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix?

To book a berth at Yas Marina, you should contact the marina management directly well in advance due to high demand. Some yacht charter companies may handle this process for you as part of their service package.

Are there special services available specifically for yacht guests during the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix at Yas Marina?

Yes, special services include hospitality packages with gourmet dining options, live entertainment events within the marina area, and shuttle services to various parts of the circuit.

Can non-yacht owners enjoy similar access and services during the F1 event at Yas Marina?

Non-yacht owners can purchase hospitality packages that provide access to certain areas within Yas Marina. These packages often include grandstand seating or trackside views from various venues in addition to dining experiences.
