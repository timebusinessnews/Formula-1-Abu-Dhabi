What is the best time to book a yacht charter for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix?

The best time to book a yacht charter for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix is several months in advance, ideally around 6-12 months prior to the event, as demand is high and availability can be limited closer to the race dates.

What amenities are typically included in an Abu Dhabi Grand Prix yacht charter?

An Abu Dhabi Grand Prix yacht charter typically includes luxurious accommodations, gourmet dining options, open bars, private viewing areas of the race track, and various entertainment options such as live music or DJs. Additional amenities like water sports equipment may also be available depending on the yacht package.

How can guests access Yas Marina Circuit from their chartered yacht during the Formula 1 event?

Guests can access Yas Marina Circuit directly from their chartered yachts moored at Yas Marina. The marina offers private entrances and dedicated transportation services to ensure easy and exclusive access to different parts of the circuit throughout the Formula 1 event.
